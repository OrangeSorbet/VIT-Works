About Veloce Racing

Veloce Racing is a Formula Student team representing Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune. Established in 2011, we've built a strong legacy in combustion category racing, achieving a world rank of 2.

Now, we're embarking on an exciting transition to the electric vehicle category, showcasing our commitment to innovation and sustainability in motorsport.
